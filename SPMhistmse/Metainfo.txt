Name of Quantlet:  SPMhistmse

Published in: Nonparametric and Semiparametric Models

Description: 'SPMhistmse calculates MSE, Bias^2 and Variance (asymptotic formulas) of a histogram at a fixed point x0. The origin of the histogram is set to 0. The true density is f(x)=2/3 *{(1+x/2)*I[-2,0)+(1-x)*[0,1)}.'    

Keywords: histogram, error, variance, origin, density, nonparametric, plot          

Author: Awdesch Melzer
